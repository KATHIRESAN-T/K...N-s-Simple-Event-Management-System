About Us

Welcome to the PEC Event Management System, your one-stop platform for organizing and managing events at Panimalar Engineering College. This system streamlines the process of event registration, scheduling, and coordination, ensuring a seamless experience for students, staff, and organizers alike.

With a variety of upcoming workshops, symposiums, cultural programs, and sports events, our platform empowers you to stay updated and participate in events that matter to you. Whether you're a student looking to explore new learning opportunities, a faculty member organizing an event, or an organizer seeking efficient coordination, the PEC Event Management System is designed to meet all your needs.

Our user-friendly interface ensures easy access to event details, registration, and notifications, keeping you in the loop at all times. You can effortlessly track your registrations, receive timely reminders, and engage in the campus community. Join us today and be part of a vibrant and dynamic campus experience!
